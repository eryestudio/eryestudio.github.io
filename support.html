❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why do I need to sign in with Google?
To keep the community spam-free, we require Google Sign-In for identity verification. We only use your email to establish a unique identity — we don't access your other Google data.
Q: Does Stock Danmaku work on other websites besides Yahoo Finance?
Currently, Stock Danmaku is designed for Yahoo Finance stock chart pages only. We may expand to other platforms in future updates.
Q: How can I delete my account and data?
Email us at stockdanmaku.eryestudio@gmail.com with your account request, or submit a request through our Privacy Policy page. We'll process it within 30 days.
Q: Why aren't danmaku showing on the page?
Make sure: (1) you're on a Yahoo Finance stock page (e.g., finance.yahoo.com/quote/TSLA); (2) the extension is enabled in chrome://extensions; (3) you're signed in. If issues persist, please email us.
Q: What is Stocky?
Stocky is our informational bot that automatically posts market updates (price movements, opening/closing signals) based on real-time stock data. In the Chinese version, Stocky is known as 股小秘. Bot messages are marked with a 📊 emoji prefix.
Q: Is Stock Danmaku free?
Yes, Stock Danmaku is currently free to use. Future updates may introduce optional premium features.
Q: How is my data used?
We use your Google email only for identity verification and abuse prevention. We do not track browsing behavior, access your holdings, or sell your data to third parties. See our full Privacy Policy.

❓ 常见问题

Q: 为什么需要 Google 账号登录？
为了保持社区不被 spam 干扰，我们通过 Google 登录来验证用户身份。我们只使用你的邮箱作为唯一标识，不会访问其他 Google 数据。
Q: 股论支持其他网站吗？
目前股论仅在 Yahoo Finance 股票页面上工作。未来版本可能扩展到更多平台。
Q: 如何删除我的账号和数据？
发送邮件至 stockdanmaku.eryestudio@gmail.com 或通过我们的隐私政策页面提交请求。我们会在 30 天内处理。
Q: 弹幕为什么不显示？
请确认：(1) 你在 Yahoo Finance 股票页面（如 finance.yahoo.com/quote/TSLA）；(2) 扩展已在 chrome://extensions 中启用；(3) 你已登录。如仍有问题，请邮件联系我们。
Q: 股小秘是什么？
股小秘是我们的信息机器人，根据实时股价自动推送市场动态（涨跌、开盘、收盘）。机器人消息前面会有 📊 标识。
Q: 股论是免费的吗？
是的，股论目前免费使用。未来更新可能引入可选的高级功能。
Q: 我的数据如何被使用？
我们仅使用你的 Google 邮箱做身份验证和反 spam。我们不追踪浏览行为、不访问你的持仓、不卖数据给第三方。详见隐私政策。
